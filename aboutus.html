INTRODUCTION

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the Upcoming Execution contractors For Interiors & Exteriors. M/s S.K Contractors Interior & Exteriors has 12 years of Existence in the market since 2007 with a strong, customer-focused approach and the continuous quest for world class quality in Indian market.

We are in the business of delivering value added, Interior and Architectural design and Execution projects for the wide profile of Corporate, Commercial, and Residential Clients.

M/s S.K Contractors Interiors & Exteriors has earned a well-deserved reputation for its track record in implementing and completing top quality project in time and with customer satisfaction.

Our strength of proficient execution are backed by experience of our in house Designers and Project Managers with rich experience in Turnkey Interior designing, Planning, Project execution services.

We have the infrastructure and technical capabilities to undertake turnkey projects in large volume. Projects are being managed by experienced supervisors along with the strong skilled force in civil experts, Skilled Carpenters, Gypsum work, POP, Soft furnishing, electrical, Flooring, Painting and Fabrication.

Using the finest material and superior workmanship to create work and living space that enhance the quality of life.
